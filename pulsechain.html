PulseChain

PulseChain is a new blockchain network designed to improve upon Ethereum by offering faster transactions and lower fees. It aims to enhance the efficiency and scalability of decentralized applications, making it an ideal platform for the PrizePool Lottery smart contract.

Benefits of PulseChain

  • Faster transactions
  • Lower fees
  • Improved scalability
  • Enhanced security
  • Environmentally friendly
